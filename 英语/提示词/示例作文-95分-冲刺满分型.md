# 示例作文 - 95分 - 冲刺满分型

**题目：** Should students be allowed to use smartphones in school?  
**要求：** 120-150词，议论文  
**字数：** 149词  
**分数段：** 95/120分  
**典型问题：** 整体优秀，有复杂句式和高级词汇，但缺少"惊艳"的论证深度或独特视角

---

In an era where technology permeates every aspect of our lives, the debate over whether students should be permitted to use smartphones in school has sparked heated discussions.

Admittedly, smartphones serve as powerful learning tools. They provide instant access to educational resources and enable students to communicate with parents in emergencies. Furthermore, learning to use technology responsibly in a supervised environment prepares students for the digital demands of modern society.

However, the potential drawbacks cannot be overlooked. Unrestricted smartphone use may distract students from academic pursuits, as the temptation to engage in social media or gaming proves difficult to resist. Additionally, excessive screen time raises concerns about students' physical and mental well-being.

In conclusion, rather than implementing an outright ban, schools should establish clear guidelines that balance the educational benefits of smartphones with the need to minimize distractions and protect student welfare.
